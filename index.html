  • Shan Easy Cook Haleem Mix
  • Boneless Chicken Cubes
  • 1 Sliced Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Sliced Ginger

Dissolve the packet of Easy Cook Haleem in 2 cups of water separately In a deep pot, fry chicken cubes using the Haleem Masala. Slowly pour the Haleem mixture into the masala. Add water as needed. Cook on high flame for 30 minutes. In a separate pan fry onions and add fried onions with the oil into the Haleem. Sprinkle cilantro and ginger at the end.

  • Shan Nihari Masala
  • 1 sliced onion
  • half cup oil
  • Whole chicken cut into 8 pieces
  • Half cup flour

In a deep dish, heat oil and fry the chicken for 2 minutes. Add half cup of water Add Nihari Masala, and then stir fry till the water disappear. Now add 2 cups of water. Cook for 20 minutes. Make a paste out of half cup of flour using half cup of water from the Nihari. Add to the Nihari. Fry the sliced onions seprately and add to the Nihari. Serve with hot naan
